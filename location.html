Haiku Mill

A botanical paradise that is private, intimate and breathtakingly beautiful. Relics of days gone by grace every corner of Haiku Mill, punctuating an unmistakable aura of romance that permeates throughout the grounds and into our “castle in the jungle.”

Villa Cetinale

Cetinale is an architectural marvel with a world famous garden What makes it unique however, is that it has been a centre for horse racing, hunting, sacred pilgrimages, and lavish entertaining; a combination that can be found nowhere else.

 Villa Balbiano

Exclusive accommodation in a veritable historical palazzo. Explore the picturesque paradise. Take a boat ride or a seaplane ride for a tour over the lake, visit its famous attractions, take a walk through the charming local towns and villages.
