Villa Cetinale, Fraser Hill, Pahang

Cold, Beautiful Sunny Days

Villa Cetinale is as romantic a place as you could hope to find, and is therefore the ideal venue to host your wedding.
Cetinale is an architectural marvel with a world famous garden,
its own chapel, hermitage, and holy wood. The park contains seven votive chapels,
sixteen full length statues and 29 busts, as well as many stone animals and monsters.
What makes it unique however, is that it has been a centre for horse racing, hunting,
sacred pilgrimages, and lavish entertaining; a combination that can be found nowhere else.

Cappacity: 350pax

Price: from RM7,500

Access to 12 Acres of beautiful land

Indoors, guests are welcomed via an impressive entrance hall with high ceilings and traditional artworks decorating the walls. There are various lounge areas with fireplaces for relaxing, a loggia with marble dining tables, along with a separate dining room with spectacular high ceilings and wall frescoes.

Be Enchanted with Outdoor Venues

Welcoming interiors honor antique elegance combined with modern comforts, vaulted ceilings top beautifully furnished rooms with original paintings and myriad artworks everywhere in the house. On the ground floor, there is a loggia with marble tables for dining and a fully equipped kitchen. The house chef and maids add a further level of leisure and luxury..
